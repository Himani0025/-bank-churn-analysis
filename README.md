## Bank Customer Churn Analysis & Revenue Impact

**Tools:** Excel | SQL + PL/SQL (Oracle) | Python (Pandas, Scikit-learn) | Power BI
**Dataset:** Bank Customer Churn — 10,000 customers, 14 variables (Kaggle)

## Business Problem
Predict which bank customers are about to leave and quantify
the revenue at risk — enabling proactive retention outreach.

## Key Findings
- Random Forest model achieved 86% accuracy (AUC: 0.87)
- Customers with 3-4 products churn at 80%+ vs 7% for 1 product
- Germany has the highest churn rate at 32% vs France at 16%
- High-risk customers (>70% churn probability) hold $X in total balance
